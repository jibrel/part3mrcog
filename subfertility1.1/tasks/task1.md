## Instructions for Candidate

This task assesses the following clinical skills:

- Patient safety
- Communication with patients and their relatives
- Information gathering
- Applied clinical knowledge

You are a ST4 doctor working in an infertility clinic. A couple who were referred to the clinic by their GP have returned for review. She has had oligomenorrhoea for the past year. She does not report headaches, visual disturbance, galactorrhoea or hyperandrogenism, she has been trying to conceive for two years. Her partner had an orchidopexy at the age of two.

The assessor will ask you some questions and then give you the results of their investigations. You will be asked to explain the results and next steps to the patient.

You have 10 minutes for this task (+ 2 mins initial reading time).
